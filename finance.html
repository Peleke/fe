What Fe does

Four skills. One agent.

Market Briefing

Your holdings, your briefing

Daily market digest tailored to what you own. Macro moves, sector rotation, earnings impact. Filtered through your actual positions and delivered where you already are: Telegram, Slack, Discord.

Portfolio Monitor

Alerts before you need them

Real-time alerts for rebalancing opportunities, risk threshold breaches, and tax-loss harvesting windows. Connected to your brokerage via API.

Trading Agent

Execute with guardrails

Human-in-the-loop execution. Fe proposes a rebalance or tax-loss harvest; you say yes or no. Position sizing and risk management are built in. No reckless autonomy.
