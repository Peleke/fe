You've been on the waitlist for almost two weeks. Here's exactly what we're building, when, and how you'll get access.

**The short version.**

Fe is a set of financial skills for OpenClaw (an AI assistant platform). Each skill connects to real data sources and real execution APIs. The agent runs on your machine, reads your portfolio, and acts on your behalf — with your permission, every time.

**The roadmap.**

Q2 2026 (now): **Fe DeFi Briefing**. Daily cross-chain intelligence delivered via Telegram and email newsletter. Protocol health, whale movements, token unlocks, yield shifts. This is live and you're already getting it.

Q3 2026: **Fe Position Monitor**. Connect your wallet addresses (read-only). Get real-time alerts for impermanent loss, validator health, risk threshold breaches, and rebalancing opportunities. This ships as an OpenClaw skill on ClawHub.

Q4 2026: **Fe Execution Agent**. Human-in-the-loop trading via eToro's Agent Portfolios API. Fe proposes a hedge or rebalance. You approve. Fe executes. Full audit trail.

2027: **Fe Protocol Evaluator**. Automated due diligence. Smart contract risk scoring, tokenomics stress testing, governance analysis.

**The Telegram bot.**

Add @fe_briefing_bot on Telegram. Right now it delivers the daily briefing. When the Position Monitor ships in Q3, you'll connect your wallet addresses here and get personalized alerts.

**Early access.**

You're on the waitlist — that means you get first access to every new skill as it ships. First 500 waitlist members get the Position Monitor free for 3 months when it launches.
