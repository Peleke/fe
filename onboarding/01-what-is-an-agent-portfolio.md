**Trigger**: Immediately on waitlist signup
**Subject**: Welcome to Fe. Here's what we're building (and why it matters).

---

Hey — thanks for joining the Fe waitlist.

You probably signed up because you're interested in AI for finance. Maybe you use a robo-advisor. Maybe you've tried building a trading bot. Maybe you just want something smarter than checking five apps every morning.

Here's the one thing you need to understand about where this space is going:

**Robo-advisors are taxis. Agent portfolios are cars you drive with an AI copilot.**

A robo-advisor picks your allocation, rebalances on a schedule, and gives you zero control. You're a passenger. If you want to exclude a sector, tilt toward a theme, or react to a specific event — too bad. The algorithm doesn't know you and doesn't care.

An agent portfolio is different. It's a ring-fenced sub-account where YOUR AI agent — running on YOUR machine — makes trades within limits YOU set. The agent reads your portfolio, understands your goals, and proposes actions. You approve or reject. The agent executes.

eToro launched this exact thing last month. They call it "Agent Portfolios." $200 minimum. Scoped API keys. And they explicitly support four AI agents: OpenClaw, Claude Code, Cursor, and Hermes.

Fe is the financial skill layer for OpenClaw. We're building the intelligence that makes that agent actually useful: market briefings, portfolio monitoring, rebalancing alerts, token unlock tracking, and eventually execution.

- What we're building and when you can use it
