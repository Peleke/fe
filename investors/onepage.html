  • ◇ AI helps with everything except your money. AI investing apps analyze but can't execute. Research tools surface data but can't act on it. You're still checking five apps and doing everything by hand.

The Solution

Fe is an OpenClaw skill that turns your AI assistant into a financial agent. It monitors your portfolio, generates daily briefings from real market data, and executes trades only when you approve.

Fe delivers through Telegram, Slack, Discord, or whatever messaging surface you already use. You talk to it the way you'd talk to a smart friend who watches the markets for you.

Trade intents are signed. Risk checks produce validation artifacts. On-chain reputation via ERC-8004 means Fe's track record is a public registry anyone can audit.

Moat
  • ▸ Built on OpenClaw, already on eToro. OpenClaw is one of 4 supported agent platforms. 30M+ users. Fe inherits that distribution.
  • ▸ Verifiable trust via ERC-8004. On-chain agent identity, reputation scores, and validation artifacts. No other retail financial agent has this.
  • ▸ Composable skill architecture. Fe skills run on any OpenClaw-compatible assistant. ClawHub distribution is a built-in marketplace.
